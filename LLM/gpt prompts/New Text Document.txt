he top 10 ChatGPT prompts that will save you hours a day
1. Simplify complex information
Prompt:
"Break down the [INSERT YOUR TOPIC] into smaller, easier-to-understand parts. Use analogies and real-life examples to simplify the concept and make it more relatable."
2. Learn from mistakes
Prompt:
"I made a mistake while practicing [INSERT YOUR SKILL]. Can you explain what went wrong and how I can avoid making the same mistake in the future?"
3. Memorize key information
Prompt:
"What are the most important facts, dates, or formulas related to [INSERT YOUR TOPIC]? Help me create a memorization technique to remember them easily."
4. Train AI to learn your writing:
Prompt:
"Analyze the text below for style, voice, and tone. Create a prompt to write a new paragraph in the same style, voice, and tone: [INSERT YOUR TEXT]"
5. Apply your knowledge TO SOLVE PROBLEMS
Prompt:
"Use your knowledge of [INSERT YOUR TOPIC] to solve a real-world problem. Explain your thought process and share your solution."
6. Compare and contrast
Prompt:
"Compare and contrast [INSERT CONCEPT 1] and [INSERT CONCEPT 2] to better understand their similarities and differences. Use examples to illustrate your points."
7. Learn from mistakes
Prompt:
"I made a mistake while practicing [INSERT YOUR SKILL]. Can you explain what went wrong and how I can avoid making the same mistake in the future?"
TIP: Improve the AI output by being more specific with your problem. Prompt example:
"I made a [INSERT MISTAKE THAT OCCURED] while practicing [INSERT YOUR SKILL]. Can you explain what went wrong and how I can avoid making the same mistake in the future?"
8. Connect with others
Prompt:
"Connect me with a community of learners and experts in [INSERT YOUR TOPIC]. How can I join a forum, social media group or other online community to share my knowledge and learn from others?"
9. Summarize complex texts
Prompt:
"I need to read a complicated article related to [INSERT YOUR TOPIC]. Can you help me summarize the key points and takeaways from the text?"
10. Stay updated WITH ANY TOPIC
Prompt:
"Help me stay updated on the latest developments and trends in [INSERT YOUR TOPIC]. What are some trustworthy resources I can follow to stay informed?"
